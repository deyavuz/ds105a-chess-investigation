Gukesh Dommaraju

Born: November 29, 2006 (India)

Title: Grandmaster (GM)

Peak Rating: 2700+

Gukesh D is one of the youngest prodigies in Indian chess. With a rapid ascent in the chess rankings, Gukesh has already become a strong contender at the international level. He is known for his aggressive and tactical style of play.
