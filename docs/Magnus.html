Magnus Carlsen

Born: November 30, 1990 (Norway)

Title: Grandmaster (GM), World Chess Champion (2013-2023)

Peak Rating: 2882 (Highest in history)

Magnus Carlsen is widely regarded as one of the greatest chess players of all time. Known for his deep positional understanding, tactical precision, and exceptional endgame skills, he has dominated the chess world for over a decade. He became the World Chess Champion in 2013 and successfully defended his title multiple times. Carlsen has also ventured into online chess, spearheading new formats and tournaments.
